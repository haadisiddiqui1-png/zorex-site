# ZOREX — Brand Identity Design

**Date:** 2026-06-03  
**Status:** Approved

---

## Name

**ZOREX**

Invented/coined word — no dictionary meaning, no direct AI or consulting competitors using this name. Existing "Zorex" entities (supplements, pharma, aviation) are in unrelated industries and pose no meaningful conflict. Domain `zorex.ai` and `zorex.co` appear available; `zorex.io` is taken by a blockchain project.

---

## Brand Positioning

Custom AI consulting firm co-founded by Haadi Siddiqui and Ali Ahmed. Builds custom AI software/hardware for niche industries that off-the-shelf tools don't serve. NOT an automation agency — the firm consults on specific problems, builds solutions, and resells them across the niche.

Target clients: mid-size businesses ($1M–$50M revenue) with problems generic AI tools can't solve.

## Color Palette

**Monochromatic** — authority through restraint, no accent color.

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary dark | `#0a0a0a` | Icon background, primary text |
| Secondary dark | `#1a1a1a` | Dark surface, icon on dark bg |
| Off-white | `#f5f5f5` | Light backgrounds, icon marks |
| White | `#ffffff` | Clean surfaces |
| Muted | `#555555` | Tagline, secondary text |

---

## Logo Mark

**Convergence Mark** — lines radiating from a central point, representing precision and focus directed at the core of a problem.

**Construction:**
- Vertical line, horizontal line, diagonal line (top-left to bottom-right) — all meeting at center
- Filled circle at center point
- Rendered in off-white (`#f5f5f5`) on a dark rounded-square background (`#0a0a0a`)
- Corner radius: proportional to icon size (≈18% of width)

**Standalone use:** Icon can be used without wordmark as favicon, app icon, and social avatar.

---

## Wordmark

**All-caps, wide tracking, light weight.**

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Text | `ZOREX` |
| Case | All-caps |
| Weight | 300 (Light) |
| Letter-spacing | 0.25em |
| Font family | Helvetica Neue, system sans-serif |

---

## Logo Lockup

Icon (48×48px dark rounded square) + wordmark, horizontally aligned, gap of 16px. Icon always appears to the left of the wordmark.

**Light version:** Icon on `#0a0a0a` background, wordmark in `#0a0a0a`  
**Dark version:** Icon on `#1a1a1a` background with `#2a2a2a` border, wordmark in `#f5f5f5`

---

## Tagline (Draft)

> Custom AI for industries that don't fit the mold

Placed below the lockup, offset to align with the wordmark (not the icon). All-caps, 0.12em tracking, muted color (`#555`), small size (0.8rem).
