Case Study — 01 Online Education

Integricept

AI exam proctoring that catches every angle of cheating.

An online education platform needed a way to catch students cheating through multiple vectors — AI tools like Cluely that hide from screen capture entirely, and phone use during exams that standard proctoring misses. We built a system that detects both: OS-level hidden window scanning and real-time AI camera analysis that flags phones, gaze drift, and suspicious behavior frame by frame.

The problem

What we built
